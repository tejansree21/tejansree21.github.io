## Overview

This portfolio site serves as my digital home — a place to showcase projects, share blog posts, and keep a running log of daily notes and learnings.

## Features

- Content-driven architecture with Astro content collections
- Blog with tag filtering and markdown support
- Daily notes / digital garden section
- Downloadable resume
- Dark techy aesthetic with custom design system
- Fully responsive and blazing fast

## Why Astro?

I chose Astro because it's content-first, ships zero JavaScript by default, and keeps all my content as portable Markdown files. If I ever want to switch frameworks, my content comes with me.
